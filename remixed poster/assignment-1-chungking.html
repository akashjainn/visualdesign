Chungking Express

Poster Remix

Deconstructing & Reimagining Through Design

The Assignment

Deconstruct a selected movie poster into its core design elements, then reconstruct those elements with a new aesthetic and message. The goal is to apply principles of composition, balance, weight, and form to create a derivative work that shifts both the visual style and intended message of the original.

My Approach

I began by analyzing the original poster's visual structure, identifying key elements like color relationships, compositional focal points, and form language. I then systematically deconstructed these elements to reimagine them under a completely different aesthetic framework, creating a new composition that shifts the emotional and narrative intent.
