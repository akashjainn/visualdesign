Visual Design Portfolio — Spring 2026

## Projects

### 1. Bad Design Gallery
A critical analysis of intentional design failures. This project explores the principles of what makes design ineffective through curated examples and detailed breakdowns.

**Key Focus:** Design criticism, identifying design failure patterns, comparative analysis

---

### 2. Chungking Express Remix
A poster remix project deconstructing and reimagining Wong Kar-wai's iconic Chungking Express film poster. The project transforms the original's romantic aesthetic into a psychological horror interpretation through altered color, texture, and symbolic elements.

**Key Focus:** Design deconstruction, color theory, mood and tone, creative reinterpretation

---

### 3. Music Poster: Solange "Neon Bloom" Tour
A digital music tour poster that combines visual hierarchy with typographic systems to communicate 30+ concert dates and locations in an accessible, design-forward way. The poster balances aesthetic appeal with functional clarity, allowing viewers to quickly find concert information.

**Key Focus:** Typography, hierarchy, grid systems, composition, color theory, information design

**Content:** 30+ concert dates and locations across major US cities

---

### 4. Tracking App: Rooted
A mobile habit tracking app interface that uses organic forms, nature-inspired color gradients, and visual metaphor to motivate users. Plant growth represents daily habit progress, communicating complex data through design composition and color rather than primarily through text.

**Key Focus:** Color as information, form as metaphor, composition, mobile interface design, data visualization, emotional design

**Features:** At-a-glance habit tracking, visual progress states, streaks and milestones, growth metaphor system
